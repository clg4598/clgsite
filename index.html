Bio

Hi, I’m Chadwick Grimes, a passionate web designer and skilled 3D artist and Illustrator with a knack for turning creative visions into functional, stunning digital experiences. With a strong foundation in front-end development, as well as back-end development, I specialize in building modern, responsive websites that are as visually striking as they are user-friendly.

My unique approach combines technical expertise with my artistic background in illustration and 3D design, allowing me to craft websites that stand out both in performance and aesthetics. Whether it’s custom UI/UX elements, seamless animations, or immersive visuals, I infuse my artistic creativity into every project to ensure a polished and memorable experience for users.

What I Bring to the Table

  • Web Development: Responsive, interactive websites using the latest tools and frameworks.
  • Custom Design: Eye-catching layouts tailored to your brand and audience.
  • 3D Integration: Stunning 3D visuals and animations that add depth and interactivity to your site.
  • Illustration Expertise: Hand-crafted graphics and illustrations that elevate your site’s visual appeal.

I love collaborating with clients to bring their ideas to life, whether it’s a sleek portfolio, an engaging e-commerce platform, or a dynamic web app. My mission is to create websites that not only look incredible but also drive results.
